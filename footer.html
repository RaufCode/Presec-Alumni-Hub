About ACCRA RESET

A Presidential Taskforce committed to restoring order, dignity, and sustainable development within the Greater Accra Metropolitan Area.

Our Mission

ACCRA RESET exists to coordinate, enforce, and support strategic interventions aimed at improving sanitation, traffic management, public safety, urban planning, and environmental sustainability across Accra. The taskforce works collaboratively with state agencies, local authorities, and citizens to ensure lasting impact.

Our Mandate

  • Restore discipline and order in public spaces and transport corridors.
  • Strengthen enforcement of sanitation and environmental regulations.
  • Support coordinated urban renewal and redevelopment initiatives.
  • Promote civic responsibility and public participation.

Our Approach

Coordination

Aligning government agencies and local authorities under a unified operational framework.

Enforcement

Ensuring compliance with laws through fair, consistent, and transparent enforcement.

Sustainability

Implementing solutions designed for long-term social, economic, and environmental benefit.

ACCRA RESET represents a decisive commitment to transforming Accra into a cleaner, safer, and more organized city that reflects national pride and shared responsibility.
